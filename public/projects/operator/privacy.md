# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 11 February 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **The Operator** ("the App") collects,
uses, and protects information when you use the application.

By using the App, you agree to this policy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Information We Collect

### a) Camera Permission

The App may request access to your device camera.

The camera is used solely for in-app functionality initiated by you.\
We do **not** record, store, or transmit camera footage unless
explicitly required for the core functionality of the App and initiated
by the user.

We do not access the camera in the background.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### b) Account Information

If you sign in using phone authentication (e.g. Firebase
Authentication), we may collect:

-   Phone number\
-   Authentication identifiers\
-   Basic account metadata

This data is used solely for authentication and operation of the
service.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### c) Voice Communication Data

The App enables real-time audio communication using WebRTC technology.

-   Audio is transmitted peer-to-peer or via secure servers.\
-   Calls are not recorded by default.\
-   We do not store call audio unless explicitly stated within the App.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### d) Usage & Technical Data

We may collect limited technical information such as:

-   Device type\
-   Operating system version\
-   App version\
-   Crash logs

This information is used to improve performance and reliability.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

-   Provide core app functionality\
-   Authenticate users\
-   Enable voice communication\
-   Improve stability and performance\
-   Maintain platform security

We do not sell personal data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Data Storage & Third Parties

We may use trusted third-party providers including:

-   Firebase (Google LLC)\
-   Cloud hosting providers

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy
policies.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

We may disclose information only:

-   To service providers necessary to operate the App\
-   If required by law\
-   To protect the security or integrity of the service

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service or
comply with legal obligations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
